Danger Codes

We use a system of colors to easily prepare for a cryptid encounter, below you will see what a qualities a cryptid must have to receive its color rating

Green: The creature is of no physical or mental threat, and is not capable of harming anyone even accidentally

Blue: The creature is not hostile, however it can harm someone either physically or emotionally on accident

Yellow: The creature is defensive and/or territorial and will harm someone who either comes in proximity to it or in proximity of its territory

Orange: The creature is aggressive and actively hunts humans and/or animals

Red: Probably not a good idea to send us instead of the military
